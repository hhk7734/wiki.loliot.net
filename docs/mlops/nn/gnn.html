GCN(Graph Convolutional Network)​

  • https://arxiv.org/pdf/1609.02907.pdf
http://tkipf.github.io/graph-convolutional-networks/
  • 그래프: G=(V,E)\mathcal{G} = \left( \mathcal{V}, \mathcal{E} \right)G=(V,E)
  • N 개 노드: V={v1,v2,⋯ ,vN}\mathcal{V} = \left\{ v_1, v_2, \cdots, v_N \right\}V={v1​,v2​,⋯,vN​}
  • 노드 피쳐: X∈RN×FX \in \mathbb{R}^{N \times F}X∈RN×F
  • 엣지: (υi,υj)∈E\left( \upsilon_i, \upsilon_j \right) \in \mathcal{E}(υi​,υj​)∈E
  • 인접(adjacency) 행렬
    • A∈{0,1}N×NA \in \left\{ 0, 1 \right\}^{N \times N}A∈{0,1}N×N (연결되어 있으면 1, 아니면 0), 또는 가중치 행렬 A∈RN×NA \in \mathbb{R}^{N \times N}A∈RN×N
    • self-connection이 있는 undirected graph의 인접 행렬: A~=A+IN\widetilde{A} = A + I_NA=A+IN​
  • 대각 차수(diagonal degree) 행렬
    • Dii=∑jAijD_{ii} = \sum_j{A_{ij}}Dii​=∑j​Aij​
    • D~ii=∑jA~ij\widetilde{D}_{ii} = \sum_j{\widetilde{A}_{ij}}Dii​=∑j​Aij​
  • 특징(feature) 행렬
    • Hl∈RN×DH^l \in \mathbb{R}^{N \times D}Hl∈RN×D, H0=XH^0 = XH0=X
